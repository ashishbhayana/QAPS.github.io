About QAPS

A Safer Quality Healthcare approach

TAKING HEALTHCARE ON CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT JOURNEY


The healthcare environment is becoming increasingly challenging. Today’s patients are more aware of their rights, demand more say in how care is delivered and seek greater transparency from healthcare systems. Healthcare organisations face increased pressure to improve operational and financial performance yet still operate within a safe environment leading to clinical excellence.

OUR EXPERIENCES

We have deep industry knowledge in both public and private healthcare. Having been immersed in the different environments of developed and emerging healthcare markets including the Canada,UK, Singapore and India, we possess more than 25 years of hands-on experience in diverse aspects of healthcare.


WHAT WE WILL DO - OUR TAILORED APPROACH

We focus first on understanding your organisation’s specific needs. Our suite of consulting services is then tailored to your institution’s goals, timelines, staffing and other requirements. QAPS health prides itself on being able to work with you to move to the next level of clinical excellence. Our forte lies in being able to: - Understand where you are in your patient safety and quality improvement journey journey and help you to get where you want to be;
- Identify key challenges, articulate specific objectives to address those challenges and close the gaps;
- Build upon existing strengths to plan and implement system-wide improvements;
- Support and Facilitate to institutionalise a culture of safety to improve patient care throughout the organisation.

We bring our expertise directly to you. Engagement is our mantra – we engage with you and your stakeholders every step of the way. From the beginning – by discovering and assessing your organisation’s current state – to final output and implementation, including follow-up review and analytics. QAPS health experience lies with helping institutions who may be facing challenges in:

  • 1. Developing and conducting a customised hospital wide Patient safety culture survey. Implement the actionable strategies to achieve safer care for patients.
  • 2. Developing customised communication tools to facilitate a smooth handover between healthcare professionals.
  • 3. Facilitating the implementation of evidence –based practices related to Hospital Prevention and Infection Control using e-training modules.
  • 4. Conducting interactive hands on workshop on Clinical Audit,Critical thinking and analysis.
  • 5. Interactive workshops on various Quality Improvement tools.
  • 6. Planning and conducting NQAS training.
  • 7. Design and re-design of clinical governance structures and processes to support accreditation efforts.
  • 8. Support with developing SOPs/Policies/Clinical Protocols.
  • 9. Developing an appropriate and sustainable medical governance structure
  • 10. Clinical decision-making platforms such as Medical Councils/Boards and clinical quality committees
  • 11. Putting in place processes for measuring clinical performance
  • 12. Implementing a medical operating system
  • 13. Ensuring standardisation and improvement of clinical practices for medical processes where inherent clinical care risks are considered to be high to manage risks
  • 14. Conducting Medical Process Assurance Audits for critical processes.
  • 15. Designing and implementing robust credentialing and privileging processes.

Our Team


Dr Renu Bhayana

Dr Renu Bhayana, a physician by background,having extensive quality assurance and patient safety experience. Dr.Renu has gained experience working in Canadian,NHS (UK) Indian healthcare systems. She is currently working as Empanelled Consultant Quality Assurance with NHSRC (National Healthcare System Resource Centre,Ministry of Health and Family Healthcare) and also a National Quality Assurance External Assessor for NQAS(National Quality Assurance Standards)NHSRC.
Prior to this, Dr.Renu was Lead Clinical Quality Assurance for a NHS Trust, preparing the hospital for care quality commission assessments and investigating patient complaints. Before NHS, she worked with the Fortis Healthcare Limited heading the Patient Safety and Medical Process Assurance Unit of the Medical Strategy and Operations Group.She spearheaded Antimicrobial Stewardship Programme and conducted critical processes audits across Fortis hospitals for continual quality improvement .Prior to Fortis, she headed Clinical governance and Patient safety programme at a University NHS Trust, conducting gap assessments in preparation for accreditation and driving UK National patient safety initiatives.
Dr.Renu holds a Masters in Healthcare Administration from prestigious University of British Columbia, Vancouver and is trained patient safety professional by prestigious joint faculty of IHI (Institute of Healthcare Improvement,US) and University of Warwick. She is a certified Project Manager (Prince II).

Sandra Subner

Sandra Subner brings with her, deep expertise in healthcare and was most recently Deputy Director (Quality Assurance and Healthcare Standards), Ministry of Health Singapore. Prior to joining the Ministry of Health, Sandra headed the Medical Programme Management Unit of the Medical Strategy and Operations Group, Fortis Healthcare based in Gurgaon, India. She led in the design and execution of various pan-India medical initiatives and programmes to enhance clinical excellence.
Before Fortis, Sandra worked at healthcare institutions in Singapore. Sandra worked as a Senior Manager at the nation’s flagship tertiary institution, the Singapore General Hospital devising clinical and administrative policies across the hospital, provision of secretariat support to senior clinical decision-making bodies including the Medical Board and developed and coordinated care integration initiatives across the SGH Campus. At Khoo Teck Puat Hospital, as Assistant Director, Sandra was responsible for heading the Medical Affairs Unit. Her work involved strategic planning, clinical service development, clinical policy development and training and development for clinicians.
Sandra holds a Masters of Science degree in Social Policy and Planning with a major in Health Planning and Financing from the London School of Economics and Political Science. She is also a certified Behavioural and Career Consultant.

Our objective is to collaborate with public and private healthcare institutions who are willing to embark on the continuous quality and patient safety journey.

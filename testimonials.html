Quality Improvement

QAPS health provides a range of tools and resources to support healthcare professionals to improve the quality of care for the patients. The tools and resources include graphs, charts, diagrams and mapping tools designed to help you plot the data around your intended quality improvement initiative. Each tool includes background information and instructions on how and when it should be used. Accompanying templates (Excel or Power Point) have been developed and are available to download and use with the own data. Links to instructional videos have also been included for most tools, to further assist in the explanation of the resource. By using these tools and resources throughout your Quality Improvement initiative, you and your team can gain a better understanding of the underlying problems that are causing the issue, gain insight as to how best to go about the improvement and develop an effective strategy to complete your project.
